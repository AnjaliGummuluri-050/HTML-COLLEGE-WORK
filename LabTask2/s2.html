Weekly Reports

Receive weekly reports on your progress and where you lag behind.
This feedback will help you make progress and understand the concepts on a deeper level.
A week's report outlines key developments and performance indicators that contribute to the success of our online educational consultation platform.
Highlight any challenges faced during the week and the proactive measures taken to address them. This may include technical issues, user concerns, or other obstacles.

Goal Tracking and Achievement:

Setting goals is fundamental to any project or business endeavor. Weekly reports enable teams to track their progress toward specific objectives.
By breaking down larger goals into smaller, manageable tasks, teams can celebrate incremental achievements and make timely adjustments if certain milestones are not met.

Early Issue Identification:

Weekly reports serve as an early warning system for potential issues. By consistently reviewing progress and key performance indicators, teams can identify challenges at an early stage.
This proactive approach allows for the implementation of corrective measures before issues escalate, preventing delays or disruptions.
